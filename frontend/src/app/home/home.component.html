ABOUT

  • Privacy preservation of data ensures that the data is only accessible by the intended receiver and prevents any modification or alteration of data during transmission.


  • In order to achieve this level of security, various cryptographic algorithms and methods have been developed and tested over time.


  • In our project, a combination of such algorithms will be tested against algorithms used individually. By doing so, we can conclude that a particular combination of algorithms is efficient and highly secure which can be used for secure data transmission.
